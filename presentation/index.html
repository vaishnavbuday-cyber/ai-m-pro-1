01

Introduction

🚗

The Problem

Traffic accidents are a leading cause of death and injury worldwide, with millions affected annually.

📊

Data Gap

While accident data is collected, it often remains unstructured and underutilized for meaningful analysis.

💡

Our Solution

An intelligent system that transforms raw accident data into actionable insights and visual dashboards.

02

Problem Statement

⚠️ Current Challenges

  • Accident data is scattered and not centrally analyzed
  • No systematic identification of accident-prone zones
  • Lack of visual tools for decision-makers
  • Manual reporting leads to delayed insights
03

Motivation & Scope

🎯 Why This Project

  • Save lives through data-driven decisions
  • Empower traffic authorities with insights
  • Identify patterns hidden in raw data
  • Support evidence-based road safety policy

📐 Scope

  • Analysis of historical accident data
  • Interactive visualizations & dashboards
  • Black spot identification
  • Report generation
⚠️ Not real-time accident detection

Challenges & Limitations

  • No real-time accident tracking capability
  • System accuracy depends on dataset quality
  • Limited prediction accuracy without ML models
  • Geographic data requires proper latitude/longitude fields
  • Large datasets may affect performance
